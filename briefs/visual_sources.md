## Site References

### Medium-Small — medium-small.com
Object-oriented design practice. Key qualities:
- Extreme copy compression — assertion over explanation, trusts the reader
- Material-specific writing: describes *how* something was made as an argument for what it means
- Single-word object naming — each piece is a proper noun, not a SKU
- Type as image — headlines at display scale function as visual anchors
- Audience-aware IA — downloadable tearsheets signal a design-trade audience

### Judd Foundation — juddfoundation.org
The non-interpretive editorial stance as design philosophy. Key qualities:
- Withholding adjectives and letting the record speak — the most confident register
- Geography as primary organizing logic — where work lives matters as much as what it is
- Prior lives of spaces as content — what was here before is part of what it means now
- Randomized accent color within a fixed system — variation without chaos
- Archival framing as incomplete and in-process — not sealed, not finished
